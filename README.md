# SUPER MARIO DELUX

### A Fan-Made Platformer

The classic plumber platformer, rebuilt with a Polish soul. New hero, a capital city for a final stage, a built-in map editor, and three ways to lose your last life — from a Sunday stroll to a total nightmare.


Five levels. Three difficulties. One flag waiting at the end.

---

# Levels

## Five Stages

### 01 — The Green Fields

A gentle warm-up. Soft platforms, slow enemies, and a layout built to teach you the controls without punishing mistakes.

**Category:** Easy Start

---

### 02 — The Old Mines

Underground tunnels, narrow ledges, and the first real enemy patterns. Timing your jumps starts to matter here.

**Category:** Timing

---

### 03 — Highland Pass

Sky-high platforms over a long fall. Wind-blown clouds shift under your feet — watch your footing.

**Category:** Platforming

---

### 04 — The Fortress

Traps, tighter corridors, and enemies that hit back. The last stop before the capital.

**Category:** High Risk

---

### 05 — The Capital

The final stage. A city skyline, a flag flying above the last platform, and the boss fight that decides the run.

**Category:** Final Stage

---

# Difficulty

## Choose Your Pain Threshold

Pick how forgiving the game should be before you press start. Each mode changes enemy speed, hazard density, and how many mistakes you're allowed.

### 01 — Easy Mode

Slower enemies, more checkpoints, generous landing windows. Built for learning the levels or just relaxing.

### 02 — Hard Mode

Faster enemies, fewer checkpoints, and tighter jump timing. The way the game is meant to be played.

### 03 — Nightmare Mode

One hit and it's over. Every enemy is faster, every gap is wider, and every checkpoint is earned.

> Not for the faint of heart.

# Polish Edition

## Same Jump Button, New Identity

Super Mario DELUX keeps the platforming everyone knows and rebuilds the world around it with original characters, a new setting, and a final stage that means something.

## The Hero

### A New Playable Character

Forget the plumber — Super Mario DELUX gives you an original hero designed from scratch for this game, with their own look, their own animations, and their own personality.

**Features:**

* Fully original design, not a recolor
* Custom run, jump, and win animations
* Built to fit the Polish setting of every level

---

## The Goal

### The Finish Line Is a Capital City

No castle this time. Every run ends in a skyline modeled after a Polish city, with a Polish flag raised above the final platform the moment you cross the finish line.

**Features:**

* City skyline as the finale backdrop
* Flag-raise animation on level clear
* Unique to this game, not reused from the original

---

# Map Editor

## Build Your Own Levels

The built-in map editor lets you place platforms, enemies, and hazards on a grid and play your layout immediately — no extra software, no file exports.

Make something brutal, make something relaxing, or rebuild your favorite level from scratch.

> If it fits on the grid, you can build it.

### Editor Features

* Platform placement
* Enemy placement
* Hazard placement
* Instant playtesting
* Grid-based level creation

## Credit Where It's Due

Super Mario DELUX is a fan project.

The core concept — a side-scrolling jump-and-run with a map, clouds, a finish line, and enemies to dodge — is inspired by the original Super Mario games.

Everything built on top of that foundation, including the hero, the setting, the finale, and the map editor, is original to this project.
