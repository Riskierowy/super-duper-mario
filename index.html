SUPER MARIO DELUX

The classic plumber platformer, rebuilt with a Polish soul. New hero, a capital city for a final stage, a built-in map editor, and three ways to lose your last life — from a Sunday stroll to a total nightmare.

CHOOSE YOUR PAIN THRESHOLD

Three difficulties, one save file

Pick how forgiving the game should be before you press start. Each mode changes enemy speed, hazard density, and how many mistakes you're allowed.

EASY MODE

01 — EASY

Slower enemies, more checkpoints, generous landing windows. Built for learning the levels or just relaxing.

HARD MODE

02 — HARD

Faster enemies, fewer checkpoints, and tighter jump timing. The way the game is meant to be played.

NIGHTMARE MODE

03 — NIGHTMARE

One hit and it's over. Every enemy is faster, every gap is wider, and every checkpoint is earned. Not for the faint of heart.

WHAT MAKES IT POLISH

Same jump button, new identity

Super Mario DELUX keeps the platforming everyone knows and rebuilds the world around it with original characters, a new setting, and a final stage that means something.

HERO CHARACTER

THE HERO

A NEW PLAYABLE CHARACTER

Forget the plumber — Super Mario DELUX gives you an original hero designed from scratch for this game, with their own look, their own animations, and their own personality.

  • Fully original design, not a recolor
  • Custom run, jump, and win animations
  • Built to fit the Polish setting of every level
FINAL STAGE — CITY + FLAG

THE GOAL

THE FINISH LINE IS A CAPITAL CITY

No castle this time. Every run ends in a skyline modeled after a Polish city, with a Polish flag raised above the final platform the moment you cross the finish line.

  • City skyline as the finale backdrop
  • Flag-raise animation on level clear
  • Unique to this game, not reused from the original
BUILT-IN TOOL

BUILD YOUR OWN LEVELS

The map editor lets you place platforms, enemies, and hazards on a grid and play your layout immediately — no extra software, no file exports.

Make something brutal, make something relaxing, or rebuild your favorite level from scratch. If it fits on the grid, you can build it.

CREDIT WHERE IT'S DUE

Super Mario DELUX is a fan project. The core concept — a side-scrolling jump-and-run with a map, clouds, a finish line, and enemies to dodge — is inspired by the original Super Mario games. Everything built on top of that foundation, the hero, the setting, the finale, and the map editor, is original to this project.
